# Grok 動画プロンプト ― キャラなしカット（22枚）

静止画をGrokに渡して動画化するときのプロンプトリストです。
各カット3〜5秒程度を目安に生成してください。

---

## 🔒 全カット共通・音声ルール（毎回プロンプトに追加）
```
No background music. No BGM.
ASMR-style ambient sound effects only: sizzling, chopping, pouring, rustling, wind, nature sounds as appropriate.
Silence between sounds is intentional and part of the healing atmosphere.
```

---

## Scene 0: フック

### 002_hook_02｜お弁当箱（俯瞰）
```
Camera slowly zooms in toward the bento box. Cherry blossom petals gently drift down and land on the rim of the box. Soft warm light pulses slightly as if from a candle. No movement of the food itself.
```

## Scene 1: 始まり

### 004_scene1_01｜キッチン内観
```
Gentle camera push-in toward the kitchen. Magic lantern light flickers softly. Steam rises faintly from a clay pot in the background. Cherry blossom petals visible through the window slowly drift. Peaceful and still.
```

---

## Scene 2A: 唐揚げ

### 008_karaage_01｜鶏肉をカット
```
Slow close-up push-in on the raw chicken pieces on the cutting board. Soft candlelight glimmers. Very slight steam or moisture shimmer. ASMR style, no movement of food.
```

### 010_karaage_03｜片栗粉をまぶす
```
White potato starch powder slowly drifts and swirls in the air above a wooden tray, settling in soft clouds. Gentle slow motion. Warm diffused candlelight catches the floating powder particles. ASMR atmosphere. No meat visible, pure powder and light.
```

### 011_karaage_04｜油を加熱
```
Oil in the clay pot slowly begins to shimmer with heat. Tiny gentle ripples appear on the surface. Warm amber glow from the charcoal fire below pulses softly. Wisps of steam begin to rise. Slow and meditative.
```

### 012_karaage_05｜じゅわーっ hero shot
```
Chicken pieces are lowered into hot oil. Explosive sizzle — oil bubbles erupt dramatically and foam around the chicken. Steam billows upward. Golden firelight flickers intensely. The bubbling gradually calms. ASMR satisfying sizzle moment.
```

### 013_karaage_06｜揚がっていく
```
Karaage chicken pieces slowly turn golden brown in the bubbling oil. Bubbles gradually calm and slow. Warm amber light. Camera gently floats closer. Mesmerizing and meditative frying shot.
```

### 014_karaage_07｜引き上げる
```
Golden crispy karaage rests on chopsticks, oil dripping in slow-motion drops back into the pot below. Steam curls upward. Camera slowly pulls back slightly to reveal the full piece. Satisfying and beautiful.
```

### 015_karaage_08｜俯瞰・完成
```
Gentle top-down drift: camera very slowly floats down toward the finished golden karaage on the bamboo rack. Steam rises softly. Warm charcoal light glimmers. Peaceful completion shot.
```

---

## Scene 2B: 卵焼き

### 017_tamagoyaki_02｜卵を溶く
```
Chopsticks swirl and beat the eggs in a gentle circular motion. Yellow egg mixture swirls smoothly. Soft natural light catches the glossy surface. ASMR rhythm. Loop-friendly gentle motion.
```

### 018_tamagoyaki_03｜流し込む
```
Liquid egg mixture pours into the rectangular tamagoyaki pan. It spreads across the hot surface and tiny bubbles form at the edges as it begins to cook. Steam rises. Warm golden light. Slow and satisfying pour.
```

### 019_tamagoyaki_04｜巻いていく
```
The tamagoyaki slowly rolls from one end of the pan, folding over itself in a smooth push-and-roll motion. Steam rises from the edges. Warm candlelight. ASMR cooking motion, slow and deliberate.
```

### 020_tamagoyaki_05｜整える
```
The finished tamagoyaki roll is gently pressed and shaped. Steam rises softly. The golden surface glistens slightly. Camera slowly zooms in to reveal the slightly imperfect but beautiful edges.
```

### 021_tamagoyaki_06｜断面
```
A knife slowly presses through the tamagoyaki roll, revealing the fluffy layered yellow interior cross-section. Camera holds on the beautiful cross-section. Soft light. ASMR satisfying cut.
```

## Scene 2C: おにぎり

### 026_onigiri_03｜おにぎり完成・俯瞰
```
Camera gently floats down in a top-down drift toward the lumpy misshapen onigiri on the wooden board. Warm candlelight. Each imperfect rice ball sits charmingly still. No movement of food. Peaceful.
```

## Scene 2D: 詰め合わせ

### 029_bento_01｜詰める（俯瞰）
```
Top-down slow zoom-in toward the bento box being packed. The food sits beautifully imperfect in the box. Warm light glimmers. Cherry blossom petal drifts in from the side and lands gently near the box.
```

### 032_bento_04｜蓋ぱちん
```
The wooden bento box lid slowly closes down. Just before it shuts completely, a brief pause — then it clicks shut with a satisfying final motion. Camera gently pulls back slightly after the click. Still and complete.
```
