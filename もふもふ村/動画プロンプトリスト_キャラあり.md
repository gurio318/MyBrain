# Grok 動画プロンプト ― キャラありカット（25枚）

Grokに静止画＋このプロンプトを渡して動画化してください。
各カット3〜5秒程度を目安に。

## 🔒 全カット共通・音声ルール（毎回プロンプトに追加）
```
No background music. No BGM.
ASMR-style ambient sound effects only: footsteps, rustling fabric, gentle wind, nature sounds, cooking sounds as appropriate.
Animals do NOT speak human language. They communicate only through animal sounds:
soft meows, chirps, squeaks, gentle laughter sounds — no words, no dialogue.
Silence between sounds is intentional and part of the healing atmosphere.
No BGM. No background music. ASMR-style ambient sound effects only (footsteps, rustling, wind, nature sounds). Animals do not speak human language — animal sounds only (meows, chirps, squeaks, gentle laughter). No dialogue.
```
